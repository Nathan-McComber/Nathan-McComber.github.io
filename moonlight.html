Ice Cream Parlor Branding

In a graphic design class I designed a logo and branding materials for an ice cream parlor called "Moonlight Creamery." I aimed to create a blend of luxury and fun with the logos and branding materials that I made. All of the work for this project was done in Adobe Illustrator.
