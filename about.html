About Me

It took me a while to decide on UX Design as an area of study, but some key things about myself that draw me to UX Design are that I enjoy being creative, I want to help people, and I like making things that people can interact with.

  • Iterative Design

Tools:

Research:

When I'm not doing UX Design, I enjoy drawing, writing, reading, and playing video games. While I haven't had any formal art education, I love sketching and have taught myself through YouTube videos and How-to-Draw books. I also dabble in storywriting and hope to draw scenes from my stories or use stop motion animation to bring them to life in the future.
