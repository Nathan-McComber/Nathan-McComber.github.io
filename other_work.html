Other Work

Here is some of my non-UX-specific work. It consists mainly of graphic design work, though other types of work could be added in the future.

Pangolin Trafficking Infographic

A digital infographic spreading awareness about the trafficking and mistreatment of pangolins. It was intended to be viewed on the web, and all assets were created by me.

Ole Bjørndalen Poster

A poster made to commemorate Ole Bjørndalen's 4 gold medals in the 2002 Winter Olympics.

Ice Cream Parlor Branding

I designed a logo for an ice cream parlor called "Moonlight Creamery." I also made additional branding materials such as a business card, a letterhead, and a menu. This project was conducted without prompting from the owners of "Moonlight Creamery", and they do not use the branding materials that I designed.

Cardboard Ghost Tutorial

A short and simple tutorial for making a cardboard ghost. It was intended to be viewed on Instagram, so some of the slides "bleed" into each other. All of the photographs were taken by me.
