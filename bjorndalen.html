Ole Bjørndalen Poster

This was created to commemorate Bjørndalen's "full sweep" at the 2002 Winter Olympics. He won every biathlon event and went home with 4 gold medals. I really wanted to focus on dynamic poses for this poster.

This project was created using:

  • Images from the Olympics (reference/template for human figures)
  • Adobe Illustrator (vector graphics)
  • Adobe Photoshop (icy texture on snow)
