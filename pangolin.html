Pangolin Trafficking Infographic

Created as a final project in a graphic design class, this infographic allowed me to explore the harsh reality faced by pangolins. I aimed for a cutesy vibe to contrast with the horrors being done to pangolins. The dead pangolin graphic is meant to "shock" the viewer.

This project was created using:

  • Paper & Pencil (base sketches for digital graphics)
  • Adobe Illustrator (vector graphics)
  • Figma (layout and composition)
