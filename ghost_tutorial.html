Cardboard Ghost Tutorial

A short 10-slide tutorial for making a cardboard ghost. It was intended to be viewed on Instagram, so each slide is square and some of them "bleed" onto each other. My primary goal was to make the instructions simple and clear while still keeping a sense of fun.

This project was created using:

  • iPhone Camera (photographs)
  • Adobe Photoshop (photograph masking and adjustments)
  • Adobe Illustrator (composition and vector graphics)
